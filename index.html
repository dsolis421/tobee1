Welcome to Rescue Family

Families come in all shapes and sizes. Rescue Family is an online resource, providing access to rescue organizations in the Central Texas area.

Our mission is simple, we strive to help find loving homes for the area's most beloved shelter pets.

Funding Our Families

Rescue Hero is a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization. Our heroes are funded 100% from donations. Proceeds help with the day to day care of our dogs, many of whom come to us with medical needs that require immediate attention.

Your donations are completely tax deductible. Become a hero with your contribution.

Family Starts with Foster

Change can be difficult, especially for a rescue. It takes a special kind of family to open their home and help these pets find a forever family. It's a critical role that our fosters are committed to fulfill.

If you have a spare bed and a little patience, consider becoming one of our foster families.
