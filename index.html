Welcome to Rescue Hero

We are a non-profit rescue and adoption organization, specializing in medium to large dog breeds. Our volunteer heroes work to provide loving homes to locally surrendered dogs. Please consider becoming a hero to a special dog today.

Ready to be Rescued

Our available dogs are ready to be rescued to a loving home. Each one has a unique story.


We take the time to get to know all our rescues in order to find the best possible forever family.


Feel free to join us at one of our regular meet 'n greet events, held twice a month.

Funding Our Heroes

Rescue Hero is a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization. Our heroes are funded 100% from donations. Proceeds help with the day to day care of our dogs, many of whom come to us with medical needs that require immediate attention.

Your donations are completely tax deductible. Become a hero with your contribution.

Family Starts with Foster

Change can be difficult, especially for a rescue. It takes a special kind of hero to open their home and help these dogs find a forever family. It's a critical role that our fosters are committed to fulfill. If you have a spare bed and a little patience, consider becoming one of our foster heroes
